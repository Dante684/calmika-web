Calmika wasn't built in a boardroom. It was built at a kitchen table, between twin toddlers' naps and long night shifts, fueled by the necessity of a father's love and the power of modern technology.

The Path We Traveled

2022

The Diagnosis

The world changed when our twin sons were diagnosed with autism. We entered a world of appointments, waiting lists, and the urgent need for tools that didn't exist.

family_history

"Two diagnoses at once. A mountain to climb, and no map in sight."

The Idea

During exhausting night shifts at Lidl, between stocking shelves, I began learning to code. I envisioned an app that was as gentle as a parent’s touch but as powerful as clinical software.

code

"Coding at 3 AM. One line at a time. For them."

Building

Leveraging Flutter and the breakthroughs in AI, Calmika grew from a simple AAC tool to a suite of 30+ modules designed for daily living and cognitive growth.
